About Us

Connect provides a platform from where you embark on friendships as an adult which can be terrifying, exciting, rewarding and challenging all at teh same time. Nothing can replace the special connections you have with those who have known you over the years, but taking that leap of faith. With our variety of chat windows to choose from get the ball rolling. Before you enter the endless cycle of scrolling a social network page try Do More. Simply log in for free and choose your area of interest, find others with similar interests and start talking. An effective advice: when it comes to making friends, “Don’t be afraid of being scared. Do it anyway.”
